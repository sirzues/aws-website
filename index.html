A song of Business and Technology

In the year 2014, amidst the bustling realm of commerce and enterprise, my curiosity was piqued. Like a lone wanderer drawn to the mysteries of an ancient castle, I found myself intrigued by the inner workings of businesses. This quest for knowledge steered me toward the arcane study of Business Management, a path less trodden as I chose to forgo the traditional halls of education, favoring the solitary pursuit of wisdom through self-study. My journey through the ABE Diploma's levels 4, 5, and 6 was akin to navigating through a dense forest, where each step revealed new complexities and wonders.

As I ventured deeper, my fascination grew with the intricate systems and structures that lay like hidden mechanisms beneath the surface of every organization. I saw each business as a kingdom, with its own unique hierarchy, processes, and battles for efficiency. The thought of enhancing even a single cog in this vast machinery, making it more effective, more productive, became my quest.

Armed with this newfound purpose, I delved into the ancient tomes of process and organizational management, drawing upon the wisdom of sages past. My hands, once unsure, began crafting systems of my own, using the simple magic of spreadsheets to weave efficiency into my daily tasks. I conjured bridges between realms, connecting client desires to tangible outcomes through the sorcery of Google Forms and Sheets, turning data into powerful spells of insight and action.

As the wheel of time turned to the year 2020, my journey took me to the shores of a new and uncharted land—technology. There, languages like SQL, HTML, CSS, and Python were the runes of power, keys to unlocking even greater efficiencies and capabilities. Though not a master, I was a willing apprentice, eager to learn and apply these new skills in my quest.

Then, as if by fate, a friend, much like a guide sent by the gods, introduced me to the cloud—a vast, ethereal kingdom in the sky where data roamed free and possibilities were boundless. In the year 2023, under the cloud's expansive canopy, I dedicated myself to the mastery of this new domain. Self-study became my sword, and determination my shield, as I ventured forth, earning two AWS certificates as tokens of my progress.

Thus, from the humble beginnings of a curious soul pondering the mysteries of business, my journey has led me to the heights of the cloud. Each step, a story; each discovery, a victory in the saga of my quest for knowledge and efficiency. Like the bards of old, I look back on this journey—a tale of transition, from the solid stone of business foundations to the ethereal vastness of the cloud, ever driven by the quest for improvement and the magic of technology.
